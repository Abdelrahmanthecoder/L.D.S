L.D.S [Light/dark mode system]

Tired of using the same online browsing techniques? Introducing L.D.S (Light mode dark mode system)... A Chrome extension that allows you to switch between dark and light mode easily while browsing on the web! Use a dark theme when browsing at night and on a daily basis to protect your eyes.
